The Performance Improvement collected a promising revenue share of the Artificial Intelligence in Sports Market in 2020. AI is also being employed to improve player performance. What problem does this innovation solve?

Using AI to analyze game footage has several benefits. It can help teams identify patterns and trends they may not have been aware of. This can help them make better strategic decisions during games, which can give them a competitive edge.One way AI is used to analyze game footage is by using machine learning algorithms to identify and track specific players or objects on the field. This can help teams better understand players' movement and the game's flow.

How to use this innovation

Many sports leagues are now using AI to help with officiating. For example, the NBA is using AI to call fouls. Additionally, MLB is using AI to track the location of balls and strikes. This information can be used to make decisions about game strategy. AI is also being used to help with officiating in other ways. For example, the NHL is using AI to determine whether or not goals should be counted. Additionally, the PGA is using AI to identify penalties.

Who is using this innovation (stars /celebrities)

Getafe, a football club in Spain, has recently implemented Zone7 into their seasons. Zone7 is an AI-driven company that takes gameday, medical, and training data points and provides assessments that prevent injuries before they occur. Getafe’s use of Zone7 has paid off tremendously, with the club reporting: 70% reduction in injuries per 1000 hours, 65% reduction in match injuries, 65% reduction in days lost. As Zone7 makes its way into football, more clubs have gravitated toward these AI capabilities, like Rangers and various MLS sides.
